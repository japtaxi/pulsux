Cheapie N15 Pulsux SSSux mods

This all started out from borrowing my aunty's brand new 1999 N15 Pulsar SSS during the waiting period of engine transplant process in R32 Skyline.

During these time the Pulsar had been driven hard everday, and has shown very clearly the car's performance is some what crippled and definitely slow in terms of acceleration and top speed (mainly due to automatic gearbox and gear ratio).

This motivated the idea of applying some simple mods to the car, in hope of gaining some more minor performance if possible with the least amount of money spent.

Below are some of the simple mods already performed to the Pulsar. These might not be of any interests to serious car enthusiasts nor would it results big power gain. The goal here is spend the least amount and DIY as much as possible. Of course there are some other mod projects that just not yet planned or started. (I have returned the Pulsar back to my aunty, this means no more mods will be planned as of November, 2000)

Enjoy a good laugh at these pages. If you happen to (unfortunately) own one of these and still remained unmodified, you can consider try out some of these mods your self at your own risk.

  • Air Filter
  • Inlet Resonators
  • Weight Reduction
